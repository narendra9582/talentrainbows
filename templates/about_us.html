This particular system is designed as a roadblock in the transcation system to ensure that whenever a fraud occurs then their is an human intervention and with the help of machine learning , an added layer of security could be added.
The objectives of credit card fraud detection are-
1)Reduce losses due to payment fraud for both merchants and issuing banks.
2)Providing an extra layer of protection at minimum cost 
 Technology Used:- ML FLASK HTML CSS JS
    
 Features
    
 ->If any fraud is being caught then it require to enter your CVV or any other feature you want to match (Fully customizable)
    
 ->Voluntarily allow or authenticate an transaction
    
 ->Add your own dataset and build own trained model easily
